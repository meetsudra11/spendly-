Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Spendly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our expense tracking service.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Expense details (amounts, categories, dates, descriptions)
  • Profile information (preferences, settings)
  • Usage data (log files, IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique identifiers)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Spendly service
  • To authenticate your account and prevent fraud
  • To generate spending insights and analytics
  • To send you service-related announcements and updates
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, and other operational purposes. These third parties are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law or to provide the services you've requested.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Spendly after such modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app
